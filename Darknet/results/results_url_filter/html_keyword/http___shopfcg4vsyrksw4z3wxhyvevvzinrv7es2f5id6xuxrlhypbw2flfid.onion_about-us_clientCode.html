About us

Welcome To Darkzone Market  About Us

 
Darkzone is a market platform founded by old darknet vendors. Obviously our main principle is about the vendor and buyer security.

Only vendors who meet our high standards for product quality and service will be offering their products. We love simple processes and great usability. We want to build an easy to use, but at the same time powerful marketplace. Both vendors and customers will get a high quality system to arrange their orders. With professional support we will assist you with all needs.

As you can see, we are always trying to create new ways to make our users safe and if our platform get hacked, we do not have any central wallets (so hackers can not steal any funds) and all user data is encrypted by default, so any sensitive or important data can not be leaked or used against our users.

Rules

As we want to offer you the best shopping experience, we have strict rules. Scammers and hackers are banned without warning. We are constantly monitoring our systems anonymously to detect such behaviour.
