Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this site?

Simple! To upload and share games from GOG.com.

What is the easiest way to download or extract files?

Please use JDownloader 2 to download game files and 7-Zip to extract them.

Can you upload Mac and Linux games?

No, this would cost even more time and money.

OMG! My antivirus software is reporting I downloaded malware! What gives?

We do not upload or distribute malware. Every installer is checked for authenticity from GOG.com and integrity before uploading.

Why do I have to complete captcha to view download links?

This is to deter bots from scraping the site and issuing a DMCA takedown on filehosters.

How are download links prevented from expiring?

All games are available to be voted on for a re-upload 30 days after they were last uploaded to guard against dead links.

Can you update or upload X game?

Please do not bug us with these sort of requests.
